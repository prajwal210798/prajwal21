Project_"E"

I guess almost every 20's kid have seen movie wall-E(2008),When I have watched movie in 2012 for the first time I thought if it is really possible
to have a robot who will have feelings?As a kid I always thought that it is better to have a friend like wall_e rather than a human and always fantasize one day I would design my own "E"

Design vs Copy paste!

"WE can actualy copy the whole code and somebody design and can build the mobile inverted pendulum very easily, but as a aspiring roboticist I wanted to "design" the whole bot from scratch, from theoritical knowledge to physical implementation , from signal filtering to control algorithms.

Its not easy!

Until unless you have sound knowledge in control algorithms and modelling of the system It is not going to be an easy task.
Some the people say it is very easy to build such type of robot but it was very hard for me because I had to explore the everything and there was no one to guide me that how can actually we design it, you would find many you tube videos and tutorials to actually assembling the parts and giving the overview of the code but maybe they would not explain or guide you actually how to do "research" and "design" things.

Infromation is Scattered!

One of time consuming thing to do this type of work is actually to find resources of your need over the internet, and sometimes you will not be able to find solutions for your questions and you have to figure it yourself or have to ask someone who has actually did some kind of related work.
Myonly reason to start this website and writing this blog is to guide students like me who either find it difficult to find resources or doesnt have any contacts with some expert in your related field.
Throughout a resarch project I would guide you through various resources which would help you build the foundation and most imporant you would be able to Save Time.


Code@github

In this model I have implemented PID controller and Only problem that I am facing here is sensor input from the rotary encoder of the wheels which is used to measure the angular speed and then convert it to linear speed.There is some resolution and quantization error that I have to take care of first.
To reach up to this level you would required to learn few theoritical things

  • MPU6050 which is 6 dof imu which contains Gyroscope and acclerometer whcih is used to measure angular velocity and acceleration, from the angualr velocity can compute yaw roll and pitch.
  • Real time signal filtering using complementary filter
  • PID controllers , if you want to know origin of PID I suggest you take a course.
